Artwork

With everything I have tried, art was the one thing that remained constant in my life. I picked up the brush and started my journey into the art world at the age of 4. As I became older, I started to join art classes. And through my whole crasy life, I never put down the paintbrush that I picked up 8 years ago, and I don't plan to. My art has been the one thing I am proud of, one of my greatest accomplishments I achieved.
